Meet Our Founder – Tarakh Soni

Tarakh Soni founded Dhan Suraksha with a vision to transform the way people save and invest. Coming from a deep understanding of financial challenges faced by common individuals, he created a platform that promotes disciplined savings in the most trusted assets — gold and silver.

His belief is simple: “Small steps taken consistently can build powerful financial security.”

Through Dhan Suraksha, he aims to inspire individuals to take control of their future and create real wealth with confidence.
