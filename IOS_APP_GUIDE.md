# 📱 Dhan Suraksha — iPhone (iOS) App Installation Guide

Since Apple does not allow direct installation of APKs like Android, we use **PWA (Progressive Web App)** technology. This lets you install "Dhan Suraksha" directly from Safari onto your iPhone 12, and it will work exactly like a real app.

---

## 🚀 Step 1: Get Your Website Link
To install the app on your iPhone, the website MUST be online. 

1. **Upload your folder** to a service like [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop) or [Vercel](https://vercel.com).
2. Once uploaded, you will get a link (e.g., `https://dhansuraksha.netlify.app`).
3. **Send this link to your iPhone** (via WhatsApp or Message).

---

## 📲 Step 2: Open in Safari
1. Open the link **ONLY in Safari** (The app installation won't work in Chrome or Instagram browser).
2. You will see the **"📥 Download App"** button I added to the top of the page.

---

## 📥 Step 3: Install the App
Follow these exact steps on your iPhone 12:

1. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of Safari (it's the square icon with an arrow pointing up: `[↑]`).
2. Scroll down through the options and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
3. A window will pop up showing the **Dhan Suraksha logo** and name.
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner.

---

## ✅ Step 4: Use the App
1. Go to your iPhone's home screen.
2. You will now see the **Dhan Suraksha icon** sitting right next to your other apps (like WhatsApp or Instagram).
3. **Tap the icon to open it.**
4. The app will open in **Full Screen Mode** with:
   - No browser address bar.
   - No bottom navigation buttons.
   - Beautiful splash screen and smooth animations.
   - Fast loading (works offline too!).

---

## 💡 Why this is better than a store app:
- **No App Store needed**: You don't have to pay Apple $99/year.
- **No Downloads**: It doesn't take up 100MB of space on your phone.
- **Instant Updates**: Any change you make to the code automatically updates on your phone too!

---

### Troubleshooting
- **Icon not showing?** Make sure you clicked "Add" in the final step.
- **Still looks like a website?** Make sure you opened it via the **New Icon** on your home screen, not through the Safari app.
